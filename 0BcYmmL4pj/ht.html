To measure BP, the recommendations in the Manual of Hypertension of the Spanish Society of Family Medicine14 were followed. BP was determined after a resting period of 10 minutes in the supine position using an automatic and calibrated sphygmomanometer (OMRON M3, OMRON Healthcare Europe, Spain). As indicated for the anthropo- metrical measures, BP was measured three times with a 1-minute gap between each measurement and an average value was calculated.
